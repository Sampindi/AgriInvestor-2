Frequently Asked Questions

How does AgriConnect work?

AgriConnect is a platform that connects farmers seeking investment with investors looking for agricultural opportunities. Farmers create projects, and our matching system helps investors find projects that align with their interests.

How are investments handled?

Currently, AgriConnect facilitates the initial connection between farmers and investors. The actual investment terms and transfers are negotiated directly between the parties after initial introductions.

Is there a fee to use AgriConnect?

Basic registration and browsing is free. We plan to introduce premium features in the future, but our core matching service will always remain accessible to all users.

What types of agricultural projects are on AgriConnect?

We welcome a wide range of agricultural projects, from traditional farming to innovative agtech ventures. This includes organic farming, sustainable agriculture, precision farming, vertical farming, and more.
